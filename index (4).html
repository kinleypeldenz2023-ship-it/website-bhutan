Plan Your Trip

Visa & Fees

Tourists (except Indians) must pay a Sustainable Development Fee (SDF) of USD $100 per day. Visa required: USD $40.

Best Time to Visit

Spring & Autumn: Clear skies and festivals.
Summer & Winter: Lush greenery or snowy landscapes.

Culture & Etiquette

DOs

  • Dress modestly in Dzongs & temples.
  • Greet with "Kuzuzangpo la" and a slight bow.
  • Walk clockwise around stupas and prayer wheels.

DON'Ts

  • Don't wear hats indoors.
  • Avoid pointing fingers or feet at people or sacred objects.
  • Keep quiet near sacred sites and avoid public affection.
